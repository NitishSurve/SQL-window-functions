Supplier Showdown
:
Evaluate Suppliers by Number of Unique Products Supplied
Intermediate
Rank suppliers by how many unique products they offer the galaxy.
Xorthax is evaluating his suppliers. Write a query to rank suppliers based on the total number of unique products they supply.

with un as 
(
	select supplierid, count(distinct productid) as uniqueproducts from products
group by supplierid
)
select supplierid, uniqueproducts,
rank() over (order by uniqueproducts desc) as rank
from un
