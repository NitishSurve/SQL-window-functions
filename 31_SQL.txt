Category Spend Royalty
:
Rank Customers by Total Spending Within Each Product Category
Advanced
Rank customers by total spending within each product category.
Xorthax wants to determine a unique leaderboard of top spenders for each product category. Assign a distinct rank to each customer within their category based on their total spending, even if some totals are the same.

with totala as
(
	select o.customerid, p.category,
	sum(od.quantity * p.price) as totalspending from orderdetails od
	join orders o on od.orderid = o.orderid
	join products p on od.productid = p.productid
	group by o.customerid, p.category 
)
select customerid, category,
	totalspending,
	row_number() over (partition by category order by totalspending desc) as rank
from totala
