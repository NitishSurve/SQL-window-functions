Top 3 Galactic Bestsellers
:
Identify the Three Most Sold Products Across the Galaxy
Intermediate
Rank products based on total quantity sold and highlight the top 3.
Xorthax wants to identify the top 3 most traded products in the galaxy based on total quantity sold. Assign a rank to each product, with the highest quantity sold ranked first. Solve this without using 'Top 3'.

with tq as
(
	select productid, sum(quantity) as totalquantity
	from orderdetails 
	group by productid
),
toprank as
(
	select productid, totalquantity, row_number()
	over (order by totalquantity desc)
	as rank from tq
)
select productid, totalquantity, rank
from toprank where rank <= 3
