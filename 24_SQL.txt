Species Spending Standings
:
Rank Customers by Total Spending Within Their Species Groups
Intermediate
Rank customers within their species by how much they spent on Xorthax's wares.
Xorthax is ranking his customers based on their total spending in the galaxy. Rank customers within their respective species based on their total spending at his trading post.

with totalsp as 
(
	select c.species, c.customerid, sum(o.totalamount) as totalspending from customers c join orders o
	on c.customerid = o.customerid
	group by c.customerid, c.species
)
select species, customerid, totalspending, row_number()
	over (partition by species order by totalspending desc) as rank from totalsp
