Species Revenue Rankings
:
Compare Customer Species by Total Revenue Contribution
Intermediate
Rank customer species by total revenue: from Draconians to Xelorianae.
Xorthax is studying regional trends in customer behavior. Help him find the total revenue generated by customers from different species and rank them within their species.

with total as
(
	select c.customerid, c.species, 
	sum(o.totalamount) as totalspent from 
	customers c join orders o on c.customerid = o.customerid
	group by c.customerid, c.species
)
select customerid, species,
	totalspent,
	rank() over (partition by species order by totalspent desc) as speciesrank
from total
