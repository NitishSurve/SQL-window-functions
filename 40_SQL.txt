Species Revenue Classifications
:
Group Species by Total Revenue into Five Spending Tiers
Intermediate
Rank species into 5 tiers based on total spending at Xorthax's post.
Xorthax wants to rank different species into 5 tiers based on their total revenue at his trading post. Write a query to assign tiers to species based on their total spending.

with finalr as
(
	select c.species, sum(o.totalamount) as totalrevenue
	from customers c join orders o 
	on  c.customerid = o.customerid
	group by c.species
)
select species, totalrevenue,
ntile(5) over (order by totalrevenue desc) 
as revenuetier
from finalr
