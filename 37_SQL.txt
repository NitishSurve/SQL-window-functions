Supplier Pricing Pyramid
:
Group Suppliers by Average Product Cost into Three Pricing Tiers
Intermediate
Divide suppliers into 3 pricing bands based on average product cost.
Xorthax is evaluating suppliers based on the average price of the products they supply. Divide suppliers into 3 pricing tiers.

with tier as
(
	select s.supplierid, s.name as suppliername,
	avg(p.price) as averageprice from suppliers s
	join products p 
	on s.supplierid= p.supplierid
	group by s.supplierid, s.name
)
select supplierid, suppliername, averageprice,
	ntile(3) over (order by averageprice desc) as pricingtier from tier
