Five Tiers of Product Fame
:
Group Products by Sales Volume into Five Popularity Tiers
Intermediate
Classify products into 5 groups based on total quantity sold.
Xorthax wants to divide all products into 5 popularity tiers based on the total quantity sold across the galaxy. Write a query to rank products into these tiers.

with totalq as
(
select p.productid, p.name as productname, sum(od.quantity)
	as totalquantity from products p
	join orderdetails od
	on p.productid = od.productid
	group by p.productid, p.name
)
select productid, productname, totalquantity,
ntile(5) over (order by totalquantity desc) as popularitytier from totalq
