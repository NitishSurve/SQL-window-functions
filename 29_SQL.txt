Category Revenue Rankings
:
Rank Product Categories by Total Sales with Tie-Aware Revenue Comparison
Intermediate
Assign fair, tied-aware ranks to product categories based on their total sales.
Xorthax wants to compare products within each category to see which ones generate the most revenue. Write a query that calculates total revenue per product and ranks the products within each category, ensuring that ties receive the same rank without skipping numbers.

with totalr as
(
	select p.category, p.productid, sum(o.quantity * p.price)
	as totalrevenue from products p join
	orderdetails o on p.productid = o.productid
	group by p.category, p.productid
)
select category, productid, totalrevenue,
dense_rank() over (partition by category order by totalrevenue desc) as rank 
from totalr
