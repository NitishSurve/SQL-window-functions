Species Revenue Leaderboard
:
Compare Species by Total Revenue to Identify Top-Earning Groups
Intermediate
Find out which species are Xorthax's most valuable patrons by revenue.
The merchants guild of Zlacksys 19 wants to identify the species generating the most revenue for Xorthax. Write a query to rank species based on their total revenue at the trading post.

with totalr as
(
	select c.species, sum(o.totalamount) as totalrevenue from
	customers c join orders o on
	c.customerid = o.customerid
	group by c.species
)
select species, totalrevenue, row_number() over (order by totalrevenue desc) as rank from totalr
