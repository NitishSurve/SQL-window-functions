Most Popular Products in the Galaxy
:
Rank Galactic Products by Total Quantity Sold and Popularity
Intermediate
Determine which products lead in sales volume across the stars.
Xorthax wants to know which products are the most popular. Rank the products by the total quantity sold across the galaxy.

with totalq as
(
	select o.productid, p.name as productname,
	sum(o.quantity) as totalquantity from orderdetails o
	join products p on o.productid = p.productid
	group by o.productid, p.name
)
select productid, productname, totalquantity,
	row_number() over (order by totalquantity desc) as rank
	from totalq
