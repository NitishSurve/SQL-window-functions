Interstellar Spending Sprees
:
Measure Customer Spending Patterns with Rolling Three-Order Averages
Intermediate
Track rolling 3-order spending averages to find cosmic big spenders.
Xorthax noticed some customers go on interstellar spending sprees. Help him analyze customer behavior by calculating the rolling 3-order average of total amount spent for each customer, ordered by order date.

select o.customerid,
o.orderid,
o.orderdate,
o.totalamount,
round(avg(o.totalamount) over (partition by o.customerid order by o.orderdate
ROWS between 2 preceding and current row)::numeric,2) as rollingavgspend
from orders o
