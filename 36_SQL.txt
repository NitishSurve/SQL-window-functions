Stock Level Stratification
:
Group Products by Inventory Levels into Four Category-Based Stock Tiers
Intermediate
Assign products to 4 tiers based on their current inventory levels.
Xorthax needs to group products into 4 tiers based on the sum of their stock levels currently available (quantityavailable) at his trading post. Write a query to assign stock tiers for each product category by totalstock descending.

with tier as
(
	select p.category, p.productid, sum(i.quantityavailable) as
	totalstock from products p join 
	inventory i on
	p.productid = i.productid
	group by p.category, p.productid
) 
select category, productid, totalstock, 
	ntile(4) over (partition by category order by totalstock desc) as stocktier
from tier
